Terms and Conditions

  • The Feedify points get added each time a donation is done.
  • For the donation of 1kg of food, 10 Feedify points would be rewarded.
  • The badges are rewarded according to the Feedify points earned.
  • The donors who have earned the badges can invest their Feedify points in some other donors so that they can earn badges. The investing donor gets the Feedify points that were invested doubled in return if the donors for which they invested achieves the badges. This helps more and more donors to get involved in converting the excess food available into useful resources.

    • Planet Savior - 1000 Feedify Points
